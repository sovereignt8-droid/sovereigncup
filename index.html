Competition Rules

Draft format

Each team drafts 5 main roster golfers plus 1 tiebreaker golfer (must be a past major champion) via a modified snake draft. Two alternate picks are added before Rounds 3 and 4, giving each team 8 total (7 main + 1 tiebreaker) by the final round.

Modified snake draft order

Alternate picks

After the cut (end of Round 2), each team picks one alternate before Round 3. After Round 3, each team picks one final alternate before Round 4. Only undrafted golfers are eligible.

Scoring

Team score = best 4 of 7 main roster golfers combined score to par. Lower is better. Tiebreaker score used only to break ties.
